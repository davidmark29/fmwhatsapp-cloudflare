The Risks: Because Nothing Is Perfect

That means you could be more vulnerable to hacks or bugs. And while it’s rare, using a modified app like this could lead to your WhatsApp account being banned.

But, hey, if you’re willing to take the risk for all the sweet features FMWhatsApp offers, it might just be worth it. You get to play it cool with customized themes, larger groups, and hidden online statuses. Just make sure you know what you’re getting into.
